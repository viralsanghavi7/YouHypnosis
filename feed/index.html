Try my morning tips:

Waking up naturally (no alarm clock ) opt to instead use beautiful and positive music to notify you. Open all the windows to allow fresh air and natural light in. I make my bed right away and this energy of getting things done flows throughout the day. I drink a glass of tall water with fresh lemon and mint and followed by my brain booster smoothie while I make breakfast. I take first a warm shower and then switch to cool water . I use scents that make me feel awake such as orange, citrus and peppermint.

Additional Notes:

Brain Booster Smoothie:

1 Banana, 1 cup of blueberries, 1 tablespoon of avocado, 1/2 cup coconut milk, 2 tablespoons walnuts, 1 pinch of bee pollen, 1 teaspoon of MCT oil, and enough water for your desired consistency.

Shower Time:

Invest in a nice shower head. I love wide and large shower heads which feels like rain droplets. Find only a few and well researched products to keep in the shower. Find scents for morning and night. I use peppermint soap in the morning and in the evening I use calming scents like lavender. Use fresh lush and soft towels, shower curtains made up of natural materials. While taking a shower imagine being in a lush forrest and the shower is cleansing your aura field with fresh rainfall.

The Art of Breakfast: Eat like a king or queen in the morning within 1 hr of waking. Use all whole foods and learn how to stay away from packaged foods. Also, make breakfast exciting again by trying out new recipes and adding your favorite fruits. Allow more time in the morning to make breakfast an event. Play music, set the table nice and spend just a few extra minutes creating quality time with loved ones.

Bonus: If you have family or friends that live very close by with similar schedules you may do a mini breakfast potluck. Set a clear standard of the time frame and allow enough time. I recommend 30 -45 minutes and time needs to be respected. To do this involves going to bed at the same time or very close to the hour every night.

Give it a try and have a good morning!

1) How motivated are you?

2) Do you have a motivational mindset or do you give up easily?

3) Practice daily scanning your thoughts for any negativity. Write down even the subtle thoughts which creep up.

4) Reframe those negative thoughts with positive thoughts. Engineer a positive feedback.

5) Be a watcher of those thoughts and emotions.

6) Make a decision to choose your thoughts and make a decision for thoughts you don’t like by using your mind to choose not to aim for those thoughts.

7) Brainstorm- Map out all of your thoughts into bubbles and draw out links or associations.

8) Reflect on your brainstorming session.

9) Revise any changes you want to make in your thinking.

10) Practice more of the hobbies or activities which make you happy.

11) Know your why? Why do you think or act a certain way? What is the underlining reasons below your goals?

12) Some people are motivated by the ego, but instead opt for what you want your legacy to be.

13) Try a new activity which may push you past your comfort zone. This activity must be safe. An example, is be daring to try your dream at living in Greece for a year. Whatever it may be.. be bold.

14) Find people which replicate parts of your ideal goal. Research what they are doing and compare the actions. Make sure to never compare yourself to others… this is different than just thinking about the tasks taken.

Repeat these steps over the course of 30 days.
